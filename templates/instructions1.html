Instructions

In this study each participants goal is to listen to rhythmic patterns and succesffully learn to replicate and play the patterns using any percussion instrument of choice or just your hands.

  • This study works best when you are on a desktop or laptop, earphones are not necessary
  • You will be asked to clap or tap musical rhythms in this study, please ensure you are in a quiet environment where you can do so
  • Each partipant needs to complete 6 blocks of rhythmic patterns, each containing 5 patterns, for a total of 30 unique patterns to recreate
  • All 5 rhythmic patterns within a block must be completed successfully to move onto the next block
  • Each pattern will be played to you repeating 4 times and you must perform the pattern 4 times as well for the system to evaluate your performance
  • Each participant will be presented with a practice block to familiarize with the interactions of the application
